Halcyon

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Halcyon ("we", "our", "the app") is a weather application for Apple platforms. This policy explains what data the app processes, why, and the choices you have. We collect as little as possible.

1. Information we process

  • Location. When you grant permission, Halcyon uses your precise or approximate location to fetch local weather forecasts. Location is processed on-device and sent to Apple's WeatherKit service to retrieve forecast data. We do not store your location on our own servers.
  • Saved cities. Cities you add to Halcyon are stored locally on your device and synced between the iPhone app, the widget, and the Apple Watch app via an Apple App Group. They are not transmitted to us.
  • Subscription state. If you purchase Halcyon Ad-Free, Apple's StoreKit handles the transaction. We receive a verified entitlement from Apple indicating that your subscription is active. We do not see your payment details.
  • Advertising identifier (Free tier only). If you use Halcyon for free, the app displays ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may access your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) to serve and measure ads, only after you grant permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt. If you decline that prompt, AdMob serves non-personalized ads.
  • Diagnostics. Halcyon does not currently collect crash logs or analytics. If this changes, this policy will be updated and you will be notified in-app.

2. How we use information

  • To display weather forecasts, severe alerts, and astronomy data for the locations you ask about.
  • To remember your saved cities and unit preferences across launches.
  • To unlock the ad-free experience after you subscribe.
  • To serve and measure ads (Free tier only, via Google AdMob).

We do not sell your personal information. We do not run our own advertising or analytics servers.

3. Third parties

  • Apple WeatherKit. Forecast data is provided by Apple. See Apple's Weather data privacy policy.
  • Apple StoreKit. Subscription processing and Family Sharing (if enabled) are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob (Free tier only). Ad delivery is performed by Google. See Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob disclosures.

4. Your choices

  • Location: revoke permission at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy → Location Services → Halcyon.
  • Tracking: revoke ad tracking in iOS Settings → Privacy → Tracking, or decline the App Tracking Transparency prompt.
  • Subscription: manage or cancel in iOS Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions, or via App Store → Account → Subscriptions.
  • Saved data: deleting Halcyon from your device removes all saved cities and preferences stored locally.

5. Children

Halcyon is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them.

6. International users

Halcyon is operated from the United States. If you use the app outside the U.S., note that data sent to Apple WeatherKit and (on Free tier) Google AdMob may be processed in the U.S. or other countries where those services operate.

7. Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, surface a notice inside the app before the change takes effect.
